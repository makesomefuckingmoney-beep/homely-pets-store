Print notes for supplier

  • Request CMYK proofs matched to brand colours above.
  • Add 3mm bleed, dielines, back panel, barcode, and legal text to flat labels.
  • Matte or soft-touch laminate recommended; gold foil optional on Competition line.
  • Website shop links each SKU to product.html?id=… with subscription & 3-for-1 options.
